Our Story, Mission, and Journey

Elevating Latino Professionals for America

Mission: The Association of Latino Professionals For America (ALPFA) at Lehman College is dedicated to empowering and developing Latino men and women as leaders in every sector of the global economy. We achieve this through professional development, mentorship, and extensive networking opportunities.

Our Journey: Since our founding, ALPFA Lehman has been the premier student organization for driven, diverse talent on campus. We focus on closing the opportunity gap by hosting dozens of events annually—from resume workshops led by corporate recruiters to exclusive networking dinners and regional leadership summits. Your partnership directly fuels these career-defining opportunities.

The Impact: We don’t just connect students to jobs; we cultivate leaders. We provide the essential professional skills and cultural foundation necessary for our members to excel immediately upon graduation and continue to advance throughout their careers.
